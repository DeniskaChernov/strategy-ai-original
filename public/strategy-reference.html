Common questions
Frequently asked
Everything you need to know before getting started.
What is Strategy AI exactly?+
Strategy AI is a visual strategic planning platform. You build interactive maps with goals, initiatives, KPIs and risks, run scenario simulations, and get AI advice that knows your full business context — your maps, your CRM pipeline, your team. It replaces scattered docs, Miro boards and generic ChatGPT prompts.
How is the AI advisor different from ChatGPT?+
ChatGPT gives generic advice based on your text. Our AI advisor has persistent context of your strategy maps, current node statuses, scenario data, and CRM pipeline. It knows your $500K Q2 goal is at 40%, that your Enterprise Partnership initiative is at risk, and that Prime Ventures is closing next week. Its advice is always specific to your actual situation.
What's included in the free plan?+
The Free plan includes 2 projects, 1 map per project with up to 3 nodes, and limited AI. It's designed to let you explore the tool before committing. For serious strategic work, the Pro plan at $29/mo gives you unlimited projects, full AI, CRM sync, scenarios, and export.
Can I collaborate with my team?+
Yes. From the Pro plan you can invite 1 team member, and the Team plan supports 5 members with role-based access (Owner, Editor, Viewer). Team plan also includes real-time presence indicators — you can see who's editing which part of the map.
How does the CRM integration work?+
Strategy AI connects to our companion CRM product. When a deal moves to a stage or closes in the CRM, your strategy goals update automatically. Conversely, strategic goals create pipeline objectives in the CRM. It's a true two-way sync — your sales activity and strategy are always aligned.
Can I export my strategy maps?+
Yes — you can export as PNG (high-res image), SVG (vector), JSON (full data for re-import), PDF (print-ready), and Markdown (text summary). PPTX slide export is available on Pro and higher plans. You can also import JSON maps, making it easy to share or back up your strategy.
Is my data safe?+
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices. Enterprise plan includes SSO and advanced access controls. We never train AI models on your private strategy data — your business context stays yours.
